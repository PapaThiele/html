Count your blessings

You turn your focus away from Jesus's sacrafice when you focus on what you have not what you don't have.



This can cause any or all f the following:>

  1. Anger - You become angry, bitter & jaded that you don't have (or have yet) what you are lusting after.
  2. Resentment - Your focus turns to evil as you try to find fault with others because they have what you want.
  3. Sinful Pride - You compare yourself to others that have what you want.
  4. Depression - You feel you will never have what you want.

  1. You spend time with Him alone
  2. You seek his face
  3. You make the right choices
