Pizza Box Frisbee

Location: Lakeside trailer park - Mayport, FL

One night my friend, Joe, ad I picked up 2 pizzas for dinner. One of our acquiatences, Larry, came by. He offered to smoke a blunt with us. We had no plans to go anywhere so we took him up on his offer. After smoking, Joe and I went back inside, finished our pizzas and assumed our Couch Potato positions . After watching the Real Estate channel and Liquid Television on MTV for a few hours, Joe decided to take the boxes to the dumpster across the atreet. He was gone for what seemed like a very long time. I finally started to get worried so I went outside to check on him. As I walked towards the dumpster I saw Joe throwing a pizza box lid in the air like a frisbee then chasing after it just to do it all again. He said "This is so much fun. Grab the other box lid". Well, still being a little buzzed, I tore the lid off the other box and started throwing & chasing it. I even experimented with folding the corners of the box up like you do with paper airplanes. I am not sure how long we were out there or if we ever got the boxes into the dumpster but it was a fun time.
